Asi shoko raJehovha rakasvika kwandiri, richiti, Iwe wakateura ropa rakawanda, uye wakarwa zvizhinji; iwe haungavakiri zita rangu imba, nokuti wakateura ropa rakawanda panyika pamberi pangu;
asi tarira, uchaberekerwa mwanakomana, achava munhu wezororo; ndichamuzorodza pavavengi vake vose vanomupoteredza; nokuti zita rake richanzi Soromoni, ndichapa Isiraeri rugare nokunyarara namazuva ake;
iye achavakira zita rangu imba; iye achava mwanakomana wangu, neni ndichava baba vake; ini ndichasimbisa chigaro chake choushe pakati paIsiraeri nokusingaperi.
Zvino, mwana wangu, Jehovha ngaave newe; ufambiswe zvakanaka iwe, uvake imba yaJehovha Mwari wako, sezvaakataura pamusoro pako.
Asi Jehovha ngaakupe kungwara nokuchenjera, akuraire pamusoro paIsiraeri; kuti saizvozvo uchengete murayiro waJehovha Mwari wako.
Nokudaro uchafambiswa zvakanaka, kana uchichenjera kuita mitemo nezvakatongwa, zvakarairwa Mozisi naJehovha pamusoro paIsiraeri; simba, utsunge moyo, usatya kana kuvhunduswa.
Zvino tarira, ndakatambura ndichigadzirira imba yaJehovha matarenda ane zviuru zvine zana zvendarama, namatarenda ane chiuru chezviuru esirivha; nendarira namatare zvisina kuidzwa, nokuti zvakawanda kwazvo; ndakagadzirawo matanda namabwe; newe ungawedzera hako pazviri.
Uye vabati unavo vazhinji, vaputsi navavezi vamabwe namatanda, navarume vose vakangwara pamabasa ose;
nendarama, nesirivha, nendarira, namatare hazvingaverengwi; simuka, ubate Jehovha ave newe,
Dhavhidhi akarairawo machinda ose aIsiraeri kuti vabatsire Soromoni mwanakomana wake, akati,
Ko Jehovha Mwari wenyu haazi nemi here? Haana kukuzorodzai kumativi ose here? Nokuti akaisa vanhu vagere panyika iyi muruoko rwangu; zvino nyika yakundwa pamberi paJehovha napamberi pavanhu vake.
Zvino Dhavhidhi akanga akwegura, ava namazuva mazhinji; akaita Soromoni mwanakomana wake mambo walsiraeri.
Akaunganidza machinda ose aIsiraeri pamwechete navapirisiti navaRevhi.
VaRevhi vakaverengwa vanamakore makumi matatu navakapfuura ipapo; pakuwanda kwavo, vachiverengwa musoro womurume mumwe nomumwe, vakasvika zviuru zvina makumi matatu nezvisere.
Pakati pavo vane zviuru zvina makumi maviri nezvina vaitarira mabasa eimba yaJehovha; vane zviuru zvitanhatu vaiva vatariri navatongi;
Dhavhidhi akavakamura, akaita mapoka akaenzana navanakomana vaRevhi, Gerishoni, naKohati, naMerari.

Vanakomana vaRadhani: Jehieri mukuru, naZerami, naJoeri, vose vatatu.
Vanakomana vaShimei: Jahati, naZina, naJeushi, naBheriya. Ava vana vakanga vari vanakomana vaShimei.
Vanakomana vaKohati: Amirami, naIshari, naHebhuroni, naUzieri, vose vana.
Vanakomana vaAmirami: Aroni, naMozisi; Aroni ndiye akatsaurwa, kuti anatse zvinhu zvitsene-tsvene, iye navanakomana vake, nokusingaperi; kuti vapise zvinonhuwira pamberi paJehovha,nokumushumira, nokuropafadza vanhu nezita rake nokusingaperi.
Asi kana ari Mozisi munhu waMwari, vanakomana vake vakaverengwa kurudzi rwaRevhi.
Vanakomana vaGerishomi: Shebhueri mukuru.
Vanakomana vaEriezeri: Rehabhia mukuru. Eriezeri akanga asina vamwe vanakomana; asi vanakomana vaRehabhia vakanga vari vazhinji-zhinji.
Vanakomana valshari: Sheromiti mukuru.
Vanakomana vaHebhuroni: Jeria mukuru, Amaria wechipiri, Jahazieri wechitatu, Jekameami wechina.
Vanakomana vaUzieri: Mika mukuru, naIshia wechipiri.
Vanakomana vaMerari: Mari naMushi. Vanakomana vaMari: Eriazari naKishi.
Eriazari akafa asina vanakomana, asi vanasikana voga; hama dzavo, vanakomana vaKishi, dzikavawana.
Vanakomana vaMushi: Mari, naEdheri, naJeremoti, vose vatatu.
Ndivo vaiva vanakomana vaRevhi, nedzimba dzamadzibaba avo, ivo vakuru vedzimba dzamadzibaba vakaverengwa pakati pavo, vachiverengwa namazita avo musoro womumwe nomumwe, vaibata basa paimba yaJehovha, vanamakore makumi maviri navakapfuura ipapo.
Nokuti Dhavhidhi wakati, Jehovha Mwari waIsiraeri wakazorodza vanhu vake; iye anogara Jerusaremu nokusingaperi,
uye vaRevhi havachazofaniri kutakura tabhenekari nenhumbi dzayo dzokubata mabasa ayo.

nokugadzira zvingwa zvokuratidza, noupfu hwakatsetseka hwechipiriso choupfu, kana chezvingwa zvitete zvisina mbiriso, kana zvakabikwa mugango, kana zvakakanyiwa namafuta, marudzi ose ezviyero nezvitanda zvokuera
nazvo, nokumira mangwanani ose vachivonga nokurumbidza Jehovha, uye madekwanawo saizvozvo;
nokubayira Jehovha zvipiriso zvose zvinopiswa, pamaSabata, napakugara kwemwedzi, napamitambo yakatarwa panguva dzose dzakarairwa, mazuva ose pamberi paJehovha.
Zvino mapoka avanakomana vaAroni akanga akadai: vanakomana vaAroni: Nadhabhi, naAbhihu, naEriazari, naItamari.
Vakawana vakuru vazhinji pakati pavanakomana vaEriazari kupfuura pakati pavanakomana vaItamari; vakakamurwa kudai : Pakati pavanakomana vaEriazari kwakanga kuna vane gumi navatanhatu, vaiva vakuru vedzimba dzamadzibaba; napakati pavanakomana vaItamari, nedzimba dzamadzibaba avo vakanga vari vasere.
Nokudaro vakakamurwa nemijenya, mumwe pano mumwe koko; nokuti kwakanga kuna machinda eimba tsvene, namachinda aMwari pakati pavanakomana valtamari.
Shemaya mwanakomana waNetaneri, munyori, akanga ari wavaRevhi, akavanyora pamberi pamambo, namachinda, nomupirisiti Zadhoki, naAhimereki mwanakomana waAbhiyatari, navakuru vedzimba dzamadzibaba avapirisiti navaRevhi; vachitsaura mhuri imwe kuna Eriazari, nemhuri imwe kuna Itamari.

Naivo vakakandawo mijenya sehama dzavo, ivo vanakomana vaAroni pamberi pamambo Dhavhidhi, naZadhoki, naAhimereki, navakuru vedzimba dzamadzibaba dzavapirisiti navaRevhi; dzimba dzamadzibaba dzomukuru dzaiverengwa sedzomunununa wake.
Dhavhidhi navakuru vehondo vakatsaurirawo basa ravo vanakomana vaAsafi naHemani naJedhutuni, kuti vaporofite nembira, nemitengeramwa, namakandira; mazita avakabata basa ravo ndiwo:
vavanakomana vaAsafi: Zakuri, naJosefa, naNetania, naAsharera, vanakomana vaAsafi; vairairwa naAsafi, waiporofita nomurayiro wamambo.

VaHemani: vanakomana vaHemani: Bhukia, naMatania, naUzieri, naShebhueri, naJerimoti, naHanania, naHanani, naEriata, naGidhareti, naRomamitiezeri, naJoshibhekasha, naMaroti, naHotiri, naMahazioti.
Ava vose vairairwa nababa vavo pakuimba kwavo paimba yaJehovha namakandira, nemitengeramwa, nembira, pabasa reimba yaMwari; Asafi, naJedhutuni, naHemani vairairwa namambo.
Vakakanda mijenya kuzoratidzwa mabasa avo, kusina anodarikwa, vaduku pamwechete navakuru, navadzidzisi pamwechete navadzidzi.

Pakati pavaRevhi Ahija aitarira zvivigiro zvefuma yeimba yaMwari, nezvivigiro zvefuma yezvinhu zvitsvene.
Pakati pavaAmirami, navaIshari, navaHebhuroni, navaUzieri:
Navanununa vake: vaEriezeri, mwanakomana wake Rehabhia, nomwanakomana wake Jeshaya, nomwanakomana wake Joramu, nomwanakomana wake Zikiri, nomwanakomana wake Sheromoti.
Sheromoti uyu navanununa vake ndivo vaitarira zvivigiro zvose zvezvinhu zvitsvene, zvakanga zvatsaurwa namambo Dhavhidhi, navakuru vedzimba dzamadzibaba avo, navakuru vezviuru navamazana, navakuru vehondo.
Pakati pezvakapambwa pakurwa vakatsaura zvimwe zvokugadzirisa nazvo imba yaJehovha.
Zvose zvakanga zvatsaurwa naSamueri muoni, uye naSauro mwanakomana waKishi, naAbhineri mwanakomana waNeri, naJoabhu mwanakomana waZeruya; zvose zvakanga zvatsaurwa naani naani zvakanga zvichichengetwa naSheromoti navanununa vake.
Pakati pavaIshari, Kenania navanakomana vake vaibata mabasa okunze pakati paIsiraeri, vari vatariri navatongi.
Pakati pavaHebhuroni, Hashabhia navanununa vake, varume voumhare, vane chiuru chinamazana manomwe, vaitarira Isiraeri mhiri kwaJorodhani kumavirazuva, kuti vabate mabasa ose aJehovha namabasa amambo.
Pakati pavaHebhuroni, Jeriya akanga ari mukuru, ipo pakati pavaHebhuroni, namarudzi avo nedzimba dzamadzibaba avo. Pagore ramakumi mana rokubata ushe kwaDhavhidhi vakatsvakwa, vakawana pakati pavo varume vane simba noumhare paJazeri reGiriyadhi.
Zvino vana vaIsiraeri vakaverengwa vakuru vedzimba dzamadzibaba, navakuru vezviuru navamazana, navatariri vavo vaibatira mambo pazvinhu zvose zvamapoka aipinda nokubuda mwedzi mumwe nomumwe, mwedzi yose yegore, boka rimwe nerimwe rikasvika zviuru zvina makumi maviri nezvina.

Mutariri wefuma yamambo akanga ari Azimavheti mwanakomana waAdhieri mutariri wefuma yapaminda, napamaguta, napamisha, neyapanhare, akanga ari Jonatani mwanakomana waUziya;

Zvino Dhavhidhi akaunganidza paJerusaremu machinda ose aIsiraeri, machinda amarudzi, navakuru vamapoka aibatira mambo vachiita mapoka, navakuru vezviuru, navakuru vamazana, navatariri venhumbi dzose nefuma yose yamambo navanakomana vake, pamwechete navaranda navane simba, varume vose voumhare.
Ipapo mambo Dhavhidhi akasimuka, akamira namakumbo ake, akati, Chindinzwai hama dzangu, navanhu vangu! Kana ndirini, ndakanga ndichiti mumoyo mangu ndichavakira areka yesungano yaJehovha nechitsiko chaMwari wedu imba yokuzorora; zvino ndakanga ndagadzira zvose zvokuvaka nazvo.
Asi Mwari akati kwandiri, Iwe haufaniri kuvakira zita rangu imba, nokuti uri munhu wokurwa, wakateura ropa.
Asi Jehovha, Mwari waIsiraeri, akanditsaura paimba yose yababa vangu kuti ndive mambo waIsiraeri nokusingaperi; nokuti akatsaura Judha kuti ave mutungamiriri; paimba yaJudha akatsaura imba yababa vangu akandifarira ini, akandiita mambo wavaIsiraeri vose.
Zvino pakati pavanakomana vangu vose nokuti Jehovha akandipa vanakomana vazhinji akatsaura mwanakomana wangu Soromoni, kuti agare pachigaro choushe chaJehovha muna Isiraeri.
Akati kwandiri,Soromoni mwanakomana wako ndiye uchavaka imba yangu nevazhe dzangu; nokuti ndakamutsaura kuti ave mwanakomana wangu, neni ndichava baba vake.
Ndichasimbisa ushe hwake nokusingaperi, kana iye achitsungirira kuita zvandakaraira nezvandakatonga, sezvaanoita nhasi.
Naizvozvo zvino, pamberi pavaIsiraeri vose, neungano yose yaJehovha, Mwari wedu achizvinzwa, chengetai nokutsvaka mirairo yose yaJehovha Mwari wenyu; kuti nyika ino yakanaka ive yenyu, mugoisiyira vana venyu vanokuteverai, ive nhaka yavo nokusingaperi.
Zvino iwe Soromoni, mwanakomana wangu, ziva Mwari wababa vako, umushumire nomoyo wakarurama, uye nomweya unoda kwazvo; nokuti Jehovha anonzvera moyo yose, anonzwisisa ndangariro dzose dzemifungo kana ukamutsvaka, iye achawanikwa newe; asi kana ukamusiya, iye achakurasha nokusingaperi.
Zvino chenjera, nokuti Jehovha akakutsaura, kuti uvake imba ive nzvimbo tsvene; chiva nesimba, uzviite.

Jehovha Mwari waAbhurahamu, nalsaka, naIsiraeri, madzibaba edu, garisai izvi nokusingaperi pandangariro dzemifungo yemoyo yavanhu venyu; rereudzai moyo yavo kwamuri,
ipai Soromoni mwanakomana wangu moyo wakarurama, kuti achengete mirairo yenyu, nezvipupuriro zvenyu, nezvamakatema, kuti aite zvinhu izvi zvose, avake imba huru yandakagadzirira zvose.
Vakabayira Jehovha zvibayiro, vakauya nezvipiriso zvinopiswa kuna Jehovha mangwanani pazuva rakatevera, zvaiti: nzombe dzine chiuru chimwe, namakondohwe ane chiuru chimwe, namakwaiana ane chiuru chimwe, pamwechete nezvipiriso zvazvo zvinodururwa, nezvibayiro zvakawanda zvavaIsiraeri vose;
vakadya, vakanwa pamberi paJehovha nomufaro mukuru nezuva iro. Vakaita Soromoni mwanakomana waDhavhidhi mambo rwechipiri, vakamuzodzera Jehovha ave mutungamiriri, naZadhoki ave mupirisiti.
Ipapo Soromoni akagara pachigaro choushe chaJehovha akava mambo panzvimbo yaDhavhidhi baba vake, akafambiswa zvakanaka; vaIsiraeri vose vakamuteerera.
Jehovha akakudza Soromoni kwazvo-kwazvo pamberi pavaIsiraeri vose, akamupa umambo hwakakura, husina kumbopiwa mumwe mambo waIsiraeri wakamutangira. Kufa kwaDhavhidhi
Akazofa akwegura kwazvo, ava namakore mazhinji, nefuma, nokukudzwa; Soromoni mwanakomana wake akabata ushe panzvimbo yake.
Zvino mabasa amambo Dhavhidhi, okutanga nookupedzisira, tarirai, akanyorwa pamashoko omuoni Samueri, napamashoko omupirisiti Natani, napamashoko omuoni Gadhi;
