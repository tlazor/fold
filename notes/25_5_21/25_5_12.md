## Thom Lazor
## May 21, 2025

---
## New Model: [XLM-R](https://huggingface.co/docs/transformers/en/model_doc/xlm-roberta)

- Compared to mBERT +13.8% average accuracy on XNLI, +12.3% average F1 score on MLQA, and +2.1% average F1 score on NER

## New Dataset: [UN Parallel Corpus](https://www.un.org/dgacm/en/content/uncorpus/Download)

- 6 way parallel corpus in UN official languages (ar, en, es, fr, ru, zh)
- human translated UN docs from before 2015
- Also a corpus with 15 languages, still in progress
