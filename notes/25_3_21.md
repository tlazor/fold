# Metrics

## Overlap
- Likelihood: sum of minimum between A and B, across the frequency bins
- Embeddings: Average of (sum of minimum between A and B, across the frequency bins), across the hidden dimensions

## KL Divergence
- Likelihood: KL divergence between frequencies of A and B
- Embeddings: Average of (KL divergence between frequencies of A and B), across the hidden dimensions

---
# Metrics
## Mean Cosine Similarity
- Likelihood: sum of minimum between A and B, across the frequency bins
- Embeddings: Average cosine similarity between A and B, across the frequency bins

# Discussion

## Mutual Intelligibility Insignificance
- [paper](https://www.tandfonline.com/doi/full/10.1080/14790718.2017.1350185) used only calculates _within_ 3 language families
- XNLI has only a couple of overlaps

## Welch's Method
-  [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Welch%27s_method)
- Estimates power spectra with a sliding window
- smoothing / noise reduction

### Coherence / cross spectral density
- [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Coherence_(signal_processing)) 
- the extent to which y(t) may be predicted from x(t) by an optimum linear least squares function
- 0 < c < 1, 1 means x(t) == y(t)
- requires stationarity
