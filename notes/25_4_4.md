# New Dataset
- [bible-corpus](https://github.com/christos-c/bible-corpus) / [Paper](https://link.springer.com/article/10.1007/s10579-014-9287-y)
- multilingual parallel corpus created from translations of the Bible
- 100 languages total, 34 overlap with FSI

# New Baseline
- [lexical similarity](https://alternativetransport.wordpress.com/2015/05/04/how-much-does-language-change-when-it-travels/)
- 69 Languages, 9 pair overlap with XNLI/56 pair overlap with bible-corpus
- computing normalized levenshtein distance from [AJSP](https://asjp.clld.org/) (10k basic vocabulary word lists) might be more rigorous

## Discussion

#### Mutual Intelligibility
- With more languages from bible-corpus, mutual intelligibility is now significantly correlated for embeddings
- note: mutual intelligence has the opposite sign as lexical similarity and fsi, which is as expected

#### Lexical Similarity
- Very strong, _linear_ correlation with fold
- present in all settings (all 3 metrics, likelihood and embedding, both datasets)
